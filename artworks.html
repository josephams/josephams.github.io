Temporal Disjunctions
The Anti-Doxa
What are the consequences of the temporal being out of sync, and what kind of spirits may be lurking? Through an exploration of the hauntological, this project is concerned with the Temporal Disjunctions that are found within society and culture today.
The Anti-Doxa is concerned with themes of cognitive dissonance, ethical bias and the oppression of noise across the socio-political spectrum. Inspired by radical care politics, The Anti-Doxa interrogates our values and questions our engagement/disengagement with the entirety of world conflicts.

The Tuning Of Soils (Soundwork for Group Ecology Project
This audio piece is situated around the topic of Extractivism. Taking inspiration from speculative science-fiction novels, these tracks imagine a distant future, where humans have left Earth to explore and colonise other planets. They now exploit other worlds and their indigenous communities, destroying all plant and wildlife in their ultimate quest for supremacy. This piece looks deep into the future, to a time where there is nothing left. Nothing but vast, empty lands. Lands conquered by machines that seldom stop roaming, searching for the final elements before parting to another world.
